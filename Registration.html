Conference Registration

Click Here for 2017 Conference Registration

Registration to the conference includes:

  • 1-  Attendance at conference plenary, technical paper sessions and workshop tutorials
  • 2- Exhibition of sponsoring companies
  • 3- Dinner on Thursday and lunch on Friday
  • 4- Light refreshments during coffee breaks
  • 5- Parking
  • 6- WiFi access during the conference
  • 7- Conference proceedings (accepted papers) and workshop materials in electronic form
  • 8- Chance to win prizes (one registrant to be randomly selected during Thursday dinner and one during Friday lunch, but must be present to win)
  • 9- Inclusion of paper in IEEE Xplore (for authors of accepted papers)
  • 10- Chance to win best student poster award (for students enrolling in poster competition)
Conference registrants will receive a hardcopy of the conference program, a name badge having the information provided during registration, and instructions for using the WiFi network.
