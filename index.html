The Texas Symposium on Wireless and Microwave Circuits and Systems conference committee invites paper submissions for the 2018 conference, to be held at the Baylor Research and Innovation Collaborative (BRIC) of Baylor University in Waco, Texas, on April 5-6, 2018.
Authors from the industry and academia worldwide are invited to submit papers of their original and previously unpublished work in all areas listed below:

  • RF and microwave circuits and systems
  • 5G and millimeter-wave wireless technologies
  • Wireless technologies for biomedical applications
  • Antennas, propagation, and electromagnetics
  • Spectrum management and coexistence
  • Devices, modeling, testing and measurement techniques
  • Microwave applied metrology
  • Wireless power transfer
  • Electromagnetic materials and modeling
  • Wireless or optical communications
  • Photonics
  • Radar and sensing
  • Internet of Things
  • Emerging wireless technologies

Published papers will be archived with the IEEE Xplore®. Travel grants will be awarded to students of selected papers to offset registration and travel costs.
